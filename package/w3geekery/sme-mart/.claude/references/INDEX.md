## How These Inform SME Mart Design

- **Vendor Profile (Plan 041):** Attachment 3 (attestations), Attachment 4 (past performance references), Appendix D (named contacts), and the administrative requirements sections define the real-world structure of what vendors pre-load.
- **RFP Package Builder (Plan 054):** The CDPH RFP structure (mandatory/scored/non-mandatory reqs, exhibits, attachments, cost workbooks) is the template for how buyers assemble RFP packages.
- **Vetting (Plan 063):** Insurance requirements, debarment checks, compliance attestations — these are the items that appear in the vetting checklist.
- **Corporate Policies folder:** Shows the depth of security/compliance documentation that vendors must understand and respond to in healthcare engagements.
