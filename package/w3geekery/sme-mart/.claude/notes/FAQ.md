# Frequently Asked Questions

###Q: what's the difference between Providers and Services?

⏺ Providers are the people/professionals (SMEs, consultants, assessors) who offer their expertise.

⏺ Services are the specific productized offerings that providers create and list (e.g., "SOC 2 Gap Assessment - $2,500", "Security Policy Review - $150/hr").

A provider can have multiple services. When filtering services by catalog attributes (frameworks, products, skills), we filter based on the provider who created the service - i.e., "show services where the service's provider has expertise in AWS."
